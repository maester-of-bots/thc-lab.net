I decided to write this website after being somewhat disappointed with Dinopass's password generation. I like the site, don't get me wrong, but it tends to generate passwords from phrases like "White bone", "Ugly Hippo", "Spicy Box", etc.. and I can't hardly give passwords like that to people in a professional work environment. Some of them are too subtle for kids, some aren't.

I've done my best to make relatively SFW wordlists, taking out any words that could possibly be misconstrued by an end user who is maybe a little more sensitive to that particular word / phrase. If you notice any words you think should be removed, feel free to email me. Likewise with any word addition suggestions.

Be advised, this site is obviously not kid friendly. I don't have any warning on the explicit lists. I plan to add one in eventually, but for now maybe don't send this site to any kids, just use it to make passwords for them.
